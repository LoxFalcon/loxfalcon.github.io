Bio

My name is Luis Regino Hernández Noriega, I'm 22 years old and I'm a Computer Systems Engineering student at Instituto Tecnológico de Veracruz, currently in my 7th semester. I'm a passionate problem solver and programmer who loves taking on challenges.

I have participated in 3 ACM-ICPC Mexico & Caribbean Regional contests, the most recent one was held at Instituto Politécnico Nacional in Ciudad de México and my team and I got 13th place!

Recently I've been developing my web skills by learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript and some other web frameworks, because I'm pursuing a full stack developer job at an IT company.
